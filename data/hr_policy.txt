Welcome to the HR portal for MinfyHR!
This document contains the company policies for MinfyHR employees.

# PTO Policy
Every full-time employee is entitled to 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per year.
PTO must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance for any time off exceeding 3 days.

# Work from Home
Employees are allowed to work from home up to 2 days a week, typically Tuesdays and Thursdays.
Core hours are 10 AM to 3 PM, during which all employees must be online and available for meetings.

# IT Support
If you need a new laptop or IT equipment, please file a ticket at support@minfyhr.com.
Laptops are refreshed every 3 years.

# Benefits
We offer comprehensive health insurance through Blue Shield. 
Dental and Vision are also covered 100% by the company for the employee, and 50% for dependents.
